About Us

Welcome to YL Edu, where we strive to provide high-quality academic support for students of all ages. Our experienced tutors are dedicated to helping students achieve their academic goals, whether that means improving grades, preparing for exams, or building a strong foundation for future success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide exceptional academic support to students of all ages, empowering them to achieve their full potential. We believe that every student is capable of success, and we strive to create a supportive and stimulating learning environment that

  • Fosters growth
  • Critical thinking
  • Lifelong learning

Our dedicated tutors work closely with each student to identify their unique strengths and challenges, and to develop personalized learning plans that meet their individual needs. We are committed to excellence in education and to making a positive impact on our students, our community, and the world."

Latest updates and features

Main class room update

A bigger projector screen has been added to the main class. It can enhance visibility, making it easier for students to see the materials being presented. With a larger screen, students in the back of the class can see the information just as easily as those in the front.

Sub class room update

Projector has been added to the sub class. A projector can enable group activities and collaborative learning. Teachers can display group activities, presentations, and other materials, which can help students work together and learn from each other.

Self-Study library

We provide a quiet space where students can read and complete their homeworks.

Board game

Board games can be a great way to motivate students to learn. By incorporating board games into their curriculum, it can make learning more engaging and enjoyable, which can help motivate students to learn and achieve their academic goals.

Automatic hand sanitizer dispenser

Automatic hand sanitizer dispensers are easy to use and require no contact, which can help reduce the spread of germs. It creates a better and safer environment for study.
